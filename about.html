Business Applications and Technologies Consulting Company

U-MAX, LLC is an Oracle Applications and Technologies consulting company and certified Platinum member of the Oracle Partner network. The company was started with one simple objective: provide Oracle solutions that are scalable, auditable and reliable. U-MAX is focused on delivering Oracle technologies to start-up, small and midsized businesses and specializes in high technology, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, online ad industries, and social media verticals.

The company has emphasis on Oracle ERP, Oracle Financials Cloud, Business Intelligence, Hyperion, Oracle HCM Cloud, Oracle applications development, Salesforce integration, and technology, as well as Managed Services. U-MAX will provide the expertise and the Oracle business solutions using industry best practices to make your development and technology projects successful.

Our consultants have extensive business, compliance and technology experience, and can deploy solutions rapidly without compromising critical functionality. We can staff our projects with an appropriate mix of on-site, local and remote resources to fit your budget.
